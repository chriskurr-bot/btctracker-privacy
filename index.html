Frequently asked questions

Does BTC Tracker send my portfolio data to the cloud?

No. All your transaction data is stored exclusively on your iPhone. Nothing is transmitted to any external server by BTC Tracker.

Is BTC Tracker free?

Yes. Free to download. A one-time Premium upgrade removes all ads and unlocks extended portfolio analysis.

What is BTC Tracker best for?

Bitcoiners and long-term savers who want a simple, privacy-first way to log purchases, track DCA strategy, and visualise portfolio performance with live charts.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. Your portfolio data is always available offline. Internet is only needed to refresh live Bitcoin price and chart data.

Is my data safe if I lose my iPhone?

Your data is as secure as your iPhone (Face ID / Touch ID). Use the built-in Export function to create a backup in any encrypted location.

Which iPhones are supported?

BTC Tracker requires iOS 16.0 or later. Works on all iPhones that can run iOS 16+.
