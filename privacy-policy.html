Privacy Policy · Last updated: March 20, 2026

1. Overview

BTC Tracker ("the App") is an iOS application for tracking Bitcoin investments. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

2. Data We Collect

Data stored only on your device:

  • Your Bitcoin transaction records (date, amount, price, currency)
  • App preferences (selected currency, reward expiry)

Data collected via third-party advertising (Google AdMob):

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA) — only with your explicit consent
  • Approximate IP-based location (country/region level)
  • App usage signals for ad measurement

We do not collect: names, email addresses, financial account data, precise GPS location, or any other personal information.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • LOCAL Transaction and preference data stays on your device and is never transmitted to us.
  • ADS Google AdMob uses collected data to display relevant advertisements. With your consent, personalized ads may be shown. Without consent, only non-personalized ads are shown.
  • PRICE Bitcoin price data is fetched from the CoinGecko public API. No personal data is sent in these requests.

4. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob — provides advertising services. Google's privacy policy applies to their data collection: policies.google.com/privacy

CoinGecko API — provides real-time and historical Bitcoin price data. No personal data is sent. CoinGecko's privacy policy: coingecko.com/en/privacy

5. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS 14.5 and later, we request your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before accessing your device's advertising identifier. You can change this permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

6. EU / GDPR Rights

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the GDPR:

  • Right of access — request a copy of your data
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — delete your data (use the in-app "Delete All" function or uninstall the app)
  • Right to object — object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw advertising consent at any time via iOS Tracking Settings

Since all personal app data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. To delete all data, use Data → Delete All in the app.

7. California / CCPA Rights

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Data Retention

All transaction data is stored locally and retained until you delete it or uninstall the app. We do not store any data on external servers. Ad-related data is governed by Google's data retention policies.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
