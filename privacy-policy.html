Privacy Policy · Last updated: March 31, 2026

1. Overview

BTC Tracker ("the App") is an iOS application for tracking Bitcoin investments. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

2. Data We Collect

Data stored only on your device:

  • Your Bitcoin transaction records (date, amount, price, currency)
  • App preferences (selected currency, notification settings, PIN hash)
  • Hourly price reference data for spike-alert calculation (stored in UserDefaults, never transmitted)

Data collected via third-party advertising (Google AdMob):

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA) — only with your explicit consent via Apple’s ATT prompt
  • Approximate IP-based location (country/region level)
  • App usage signals for ad measurement

We do not collect: names, email addresses, financial account data, precise GPS location, or any other personal information.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • LOCAL Transaction and preference data stays on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.
  • PRICE Bitcoin price data is fetched from public APIs (CoinGecko, Bitstamp, Kraken, Binance, Coinbase, Stooq). No personal data is included in these requests.
  • ALERTS Market update notifications (daily briefing and hourly spike alerts) are generated locally on your device. No personal data is sent to any server to deliver these notifications.
  • ADS Google AdMob uses collected data to display relevant advertisements. With your consent, personalized ads may be shown. Without consent, only non-personalized ads are shown.

4. Background App Refresh

When you enable Market Updates in the app, BTC Tracker uses iOS Background App Refresh to periodically fetch the current Bitcoin price — even when the app is not open. This allows the app to deliver hourly spike alerts (±2% price change within 60 minutes) without requiring you to open the app.

What is fetched: only the current BTC price from a public cryptocurrency API. No personal data is sent.

How to disable: Go to iOS Settings → General → Background App Refresh and disable it for BTC Tracker. You can also turn off Market Updates entirely in the app under Settings → Display & Alerts.

5. Push Notifications

BTC Tracker can send the following optional local push notifications, generated entirely on your device:

  • Daily Briefing: a morning summary of the current BTC price, scheduled locally at 9:00 am.
  • Hourly Spike Alert: a notification when BTC moves more than ±2% within a 60-minute window.
  • Price Target Alert: a notification when the live BTC price crosses your personal buy or sell target.

All notifications are generated locally using iOS UserNotifications framework. No notification content or delivery data is sent to any server. You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings → Notifications → BTC Tracker, or from within the app.

6. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob — provides advertising services. Google’s privacy policy applies to their data collection: policies.google.com/privacy

Price Data APIs — the app fetches public BTC price data from the following sources. No personal data is sent to any of these services:

  • CoinGecko — coingecko.com/en/privacy
  • Bitstamp — bitstamp.net/privacy-policy
  • Kraken — kraken.com/legal/privacy
  • Binance — binance.com/en/privacy
  • Coinbase — coinbase.com/legal/privacy
  • Stooq — historical price data, no personal data transmitted

7. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS 14.5 and later, we request your permission via Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before accessing your device’s advertising identifier. You can change this permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

8. EU / GDPR Rights

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the GDPR:

  • Right of access — request a copy of your data
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — delete your data via the in-app “Delete All” function or by uninstalling the app
  • Right to object — object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw advertising consent at any time via iOS Tracking Settings

Since all personal app data is stored locally on your device, you have full control at all times.

9. California / CCPA Rights

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at the address below.

10. Data Retention

All transaction data is stored locally and retained until you delete it or uninstall the app. We do not store any data on external servers. Ad-related data is governed by Google’s data retention policies.

11. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date at the top. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
