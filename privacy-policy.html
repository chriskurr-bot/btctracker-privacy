3. Data Stored on Your Device (Not Transmitted)

The following data is stored exclusively on your device in local JSON files and is never transmitted to any server operated by us:

  • Bitcoin transaction records (date, amount, BTC price, currency, optional comment)
  • App preferences (selected display currency, PIN lock settings)
  • Cached Bitcoin price history (daily EUR/USD prices from public APIs)
  • Rewarded-ad unlock expiry timestamp
  • Premium purchase status (stored in iOS Keychain)
  • PIN code hash (stored in iOS Keychain – never leaves your device)

You can delete all local data at any time via Data → Delete All in the app, or by uninstalling the app.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or account credentials
  • Precise GPS or location data
  • Bank account or payment card information
  • Health or biometric data
  • Contacts, photos, or camera data
  • Browsing history or cross-app activity
  • Any data from children under 13 (or 16 in the EU)

6. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS 14.5 and later, we request your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before AdMob can access your device's advertising identifier (IDFA). You can change this permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

If you deny tracking, only non-personalized advertisements will be displayed.

7. EU / GDPR Rights

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the GDPR:

  • Right of access — request a copy of your data
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — delete your data (use in-app Delete All or uninstall)
  • Right to object — object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to withdraw consent — revoke advertising consent at any time via iOS Tracking Settings

Since all personal app data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times.

The Google GDPR consent dialog (IAB TCF v2) is displayed to users in the EU/EEA/UK/Switzerland before any advertising data is collected.

8. California / CCPA Rights

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at the email address below.

9. Data Retention

All transaction and preference data is stored locally and retained until you delete it or uninstall the app. We do not store any data on external servers. Ad-related data collected by Google AdMob is governed by Google's data retention policies.

10. Security

Sensitive data (PIN code, premium purchase status) is stored in the iOS Keychain with kSecAttrAccessibleWhenUnlockedThisDeviceOnly — it is not backed up to iCloud and is not transferred to new devices. All network communication uses HTTPS/TLS. The app supports optional PIN protection and Face ID / Touch ID unlock.

Transaction data stored in the app's local Documents directory follows iOS default backup behavior: if you have iCloud Backup enabled in your iPhone Settings, this data is included in your device backup alongside other app data. You can manage this under Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → iCloud Backup.

11. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EU) and is rated 12+ on the Apple App Store. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
