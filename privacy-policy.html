Privacy Policy · Last updated: March 20, 2026

1. Overview

BTC Tracker is an iOS application for tracking Bitcoin investments. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

Stored only on your device: Bitcoin transaction records, app preferences (currency, reward expiry).

Via Google AdMob (with consent): Device advertising identifier (IDFA), approximate IP-based location, app usage signals.

Not collected: Names, email addresses, financial account data, precise GPS location.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • LOCAL Transaction data stays on device, never transmitted to us.
  • ADS AdMob shows relevant ads. With consent: personalized. Without: non-personalized.
  • PRICE BTC prices fetched from CoinGecko API. No personal data sent.

4. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob: policies.google.com/privacy

CoinGecko API: coingecko.com/en/privacy

5. App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14.5+, we request ATT permission before accessing IDFA. Change anytime: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

6. EU / GDPR Rights

  • Right of access — request a copy of your data
  • Right to erasure — use in-app "Delete All" or uninstall
  • Right to withdraw consent — via iOS Tracking Settings

7. California / CCPA Rights

California residents may request disclosure, deletion, or opt-out of data sale. We do not sell personal data. Contact us below.

8. Children's Privacy

Not directed to children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

We may update this policy. Changes reflected by "Last updated" date. Continued use = acceptance.
